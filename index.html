About me

Environmental design engineering.

An MSc Light and Lighting student at University College London, with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. My academic interests lie at the intersection of lighting design, indoor environmental quality, and human wellbeing. I am particularly interested in how personal daylight exposure, environmental conditions, and spatial experience can be measured and translated into healthier design strategies for the built environment. My work combines technical analysis, environmental performance evaluation, and human-centred research, with a broader aim of contributing to evidence-based approaches to sustainable and wellbeing-oriented design.
